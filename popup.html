This bot helps you buy any product from amazon as soon as it is available. You'll just have to start this bot while on the amazon product page and the AmazonBot will start checking every 30 seconds(default) and as soon as the product is available , the Bot will add to the card, will select the first delivery address and take you to the payment screen. if the payment screen has your Credit Card as the first option and you enter CVV when starting the bot. The bot will enter the CVV and take you to the page where you can enter your OTP.
